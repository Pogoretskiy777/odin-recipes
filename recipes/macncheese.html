Mac N Cheese

Quick way to make a really rich and creamy mac 'n cheese using alfredo sauce in place of the bechamel.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound rotini pasta
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
  • 3/4 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons shredded Cheddar cheese, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  3. Cook the rotini at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
  4. Drain pasta and return to pot.
  5. Stir Alfredo sauce and 3/4 pound Cheddar cheese into pasta until cheese begins to melt.
  6. Pour pasta mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Top with bread crumbs and 2 tablespoons Cheddar cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bread crumbs are golden brown, about 30 minutes.
